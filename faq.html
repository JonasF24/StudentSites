Ordering

Fill out our order form — it takes about 5–7 minutes. Tell us about your activities, projects, and goals, and choose your package. We'll send a payment link and get started once you've paid.
The form covers everything: your bio, activities, awards, work/volunteer experience, skills, projects (with optional YouTube links), social media profiles, and design preferences. If you have a photo you'd like included, you can upload it in the form too.
Simple ($38) — best if you want a single clean page to list your background and skills. Great for high school students applying to college.

Recommended ($56) — most popular. A 3-page site with dedicated project showcase. Perfect for college students applying to internships.

Premium ($74) — for students with a lot to show: up to 5 pages, scroll animations, and guides for getting a custom domain.

Not at all. You fill out the form, we build everything, and you get a step-by-step guide with screenshots for putting it online. No coding experience needed at any point.

Delivery

We'll email you the finished files (HTML, CSS, JS) within 3–5 business days of receiving payment. The email also includes your step-by-step deployment guide for GitHub Pages.
3–5 business days after payment is confirmed. During peak season (August–November and January–March) it may be closer to 5 days. If you have a specific deadline, mention it in the "Additional notes" field of the order form and we'll do our best to accommodate.
You receive a ZIP file containing all website files (HTML, CSS, JavaScript), a plain text delivery note with deployment instructions, and — depending on your plan — a GitHub Pages deploy guide, GitHub Education Pack guide, and/or custom domain setup guide.

Revisions

A revision is a change to existing content: updating text, swapping a project description, adjusting colors within your chosen scheme, correcting a typo, or changing a link. Adding entirely new pages, new sections, or requesting a redesign is not included in revisions but can be quoted separately.
Reply to the delivery email with your requested changes clearly listed. We'll turn around revisions within 2–3 business days. Each order includes 5 free revision rounds.
Your 5 free revisions are valid for 60 days from delivery. After that, content updates fall under the Quarterly Update Service ($20/quarter) or can be quoted individually.

Hosting & domains

We recommend GitHub Pages — it's completely free and reliable. Your site will live at yourname.github.io. The deployment guide we include walks you through every step, even if you've never used GitHub before.
Yes. Recommended and Premium plans include two guides:

GitHub Education Pack — if you have a .edu email, you can get a free .me domain through Namecheap's student offer.

Custom domain guide — if you'd rather buy your own .com or .dev, domains typically cost $10–15/year from Namecheap or Cloudflare. We walk you through setup step by step.

We don't resell or register domains ourselves.
None from us — you pay once and own the code. GitHub Pages hosting is free forever. The only possible ongoing cost is if you choose a custom domain from a registrar (~$10–15/yr), which is entirely optional.

Payment

After you submit the order form, we'll email you a Stripe payment link. We accept all major credit and debit cards. Work begins once payment is confirmed.
If we haven't started work yet, we'll issue a full refund. Once work has begun, refunds are not available — but we'll use all 5 revision rounds to make sure you're happy with the result. If you have concerns, email us before ordering and we're happy to answer any questions.
No — the 25% launch discount is available for a limited time while we're just getting started. Once the sale ends, prices will return to $50 / $75 / $99. If you order during the sale, you lock in the discounted price.

Quarterly updates

Four times a year (January, April, July, October) we'll reach out to collect your latest updates — new projects, changed achievements, or a refreshed bio. We rebuild and send you updated files within 3–5 business days. This is content changes only; full redesigns are not included.
Yes — if you need a one-off update outside the quarterly schedule, email us and we can quote a single update. Typically $10–15 depending on the scope of changes.
